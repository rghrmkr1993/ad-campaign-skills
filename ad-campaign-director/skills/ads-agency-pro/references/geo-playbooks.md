# Geo Playbooks — Any Market in the World

This file has three parts:
1. **The Geo Framework** — a repeatable method to build a playbook for ANY market.
2. **Regional quick guides** — starting points for every world region.
3. **Worked examples** — six fully-built market playbooks showing the target depth.

---

# Part 1 — The Geo Framework (use for any market)

Before planning media for a new market, answer these seven questions. The answers ARE the
playbook.

## 1. Platform availability & legal status
- Is every planned platform actually available? (e.g., **TikTok is banned in India**;
  Meta/Google/YouTube are blocked in mainland China; some platforms are restricted in
  specific countries.) Check current status at planning time — bans change.
- Are there ad-category restrictions in this country (alcohol, gambling, health, finance,
  political)?

## 2. Language map
- What languages do people BUY in (often different from official languages)?
- One language = one ad set/ad group. Never mix languages in a single ad set — it muddies
  creative signal and reporting.
- Does the market punish non-native copy? (High-context markets like Japan punish it hard.)

## 3. Payment & commerce mechanics
- Dominant payment rails (cards, wallets, UPI/Pix-style instant payments, COD, bank transfer).
- COD share — high-COD markets need order-confirmation flows (WhatsApp confirmation cuts
  COD refusal rates).
- Where does buying actually happen: brand site, marketplaces (Amazon, Shopee, Mercado
  Libre), social commerce (WhatsApp/IG DM), or live shopping?

## 4. Seasonal & festival calendar
- List the 5-8 commercial peaks (religious festivals, national sales events like 11.11/BFCM,
  wedding/gifting seasons, back-to-school).
- Peaks inflate CPMs (2-3x at the biggest) — lock creative 4-6 weeks ahead, never start a
  learning phase inside a peak.

## 5. Privacy & compliance regime
- Data law (GDPR, CCPA/state laws, DPDP, PDPA, LGPD…) → consent mode, lead-form consent
  language, retargeting limits.
- Ad-standards body (ASA, ASCI, FTC…) → claims substantiation, influencer disclosure rules.

## 6. CPM tier & testing strategy
- Classify the market: LOW-CPM (cheap reach — good for creative testing), MID, PREMIUM
  (expensive — arrive with pre-validated creative).
- **Test cheap, scale expensive:** when audiences overlap (diaspora chains, shared
  languages), validate concepts in the low-CPM market first.

## 7. Creator & content ecosystem
- Which creator tier is affordable and trusted here? Is there a strong meme/UGC culture?
- Platform-native content codes: what does "native" look like in this market's feed?

# Part 2 — Regional quick guides

Starting points per region. Verify facts at planning time; markets move.

## North America (US, Canada)
- Highest CPM tier globally (Meta often $10-40+; Q4/BFCM spikes hard). Arrive with
  validated creative.
- UGC/creator-led is table stakes; whitelisted creator (partnership) ads scale.
- CTV/YouTube-on-TV is a major surface — 30-60s cinematic assets warranted.
- Privacy: ATT signal loss on iOS, US state laws (CA/CO/VA…), Canada PIPEDA/Quebec Law 25 —
  CAPI + enhanced conversions + first-party data are mandatory infrastructure.
- Canada: French creative required for Quebec.

## Latin America (Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia…)
- WhatsApp is the dominant communication AND commerce channel — click-to-WhatsApp ads are a
  primary conversion path, not a gimmick.
- Brazil: Pix instant payments transformed ecom; Mercado Libre is the marketplace gravity
  well; LGPD is the data law; Portuguese ≠ Spanish — separate everything.
- Mid-to-low CPM tier; strong meme/creator culture; TikTok + Instagram both huge.
- Peaks: Black Friday (massive in Brazil), Hot Sale (MX), Mother's Day (top-3 retail moment
  across LatAm), Christmas.

## Western & Central Europe (EU + UK)
- GDPR + ePrivacy: Google Consent Mode v2 required; cookie-consent quality directly affects
  signal; CAPI/enhanced conversions essential.
- One campaign per country-language; even "English-friendly" markets (NL, Nordics) convert
  better in local language for mass audiences.
- UK: ASA/CAP Code strict on beauty/health claims and influencer filters; HFSS food rules.
- DACH: quality/compliance-sensitive; trust badges lift CVR. France: loi Evin (alcohol),
  strict promo rules. Premium CPM tier overall.
- Peaks: BFCM (now pan-EU), January sales, back-to-school, Christmas.

## Middle East & GCC (UAE, KSA, Qatar, Kuwait…)
- **Ramadan → Eid is the mega season** (lunar calendar — recompute dates yearly): night-time
  viewing explodes, CPMs spike, gifting/food/fashion budgets concentrate.
- Arabic creative: RTL layouts; dialect matters (Gulf vs Levantine vs Egyptian); English
  works for expat segments in UAE.
- Platform mix skews: Snapchat and TikTok unusually strong in KSA; Instagram dominant in UAE.
- Premium CPM tier in UAE/KSA. Large South Asian expat audiences — target in their own
  languages for remittance-linked purchases.
- Conservative creative review: modesty norms; no alcohol/dating in most markets.

## Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Egypt…)
- Mobile-first, data-cost-sensitive: light creative files, short videos, offline-friendly
  funnels (WhatsApp, call buttons).
- Payments: M-Pesa (Kenya) and mobile money across East/West Africa; COD common; card
  penetration low outside South Africa.
- Facebook + WhatsApp dominate; TikTok growing fast with young populations.
- Low-CPM tier — excellent creative-testing ground for global English concepts.
- Delivery-reliability messaging lifts conversion more than discounting.

## South Asia (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal)
- India: full worked example below — regional-language strategy is the core edge; **TikTok
  banned**; UPI + COD; festival calendar drives everything.
- Pakistan/Bangladesh: Facebook-first mass reach, low CPMs, COD-dominant ecom, Ramadan/Eid
  + cricket-season peaks.
- Sri Lanka: worked example below.

## Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore)
- TikTok's strongest commerce region: TikTok Shop + GMV Max + affiliate creators + Live
  Shopping are mainstream retail, led by Indonesia.
- Wallet map: GoPay/OVO (ID), GCash/Maya (PH), TrueMoney (TH), MoMo/ZaloPay (VN),
  Touch 'n Go (MY), GrabPay (regional).
- Marketplace events rule the calendar: 9.9, 10.10, 11.11, 12.12 + Ramadan/Raya (ID/MY).
- Low-to-mid CPM tier ($1-5 TikTok CPMs) — test-and-scale paradise. Localize per market:
  language, humor, trending sounds. MY and SG worked examples below.

## East Asia (Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong)
- Japan: highest creative-quality bar; LINE + Yahoo! Japan matter alongside Google/Meta;
  machine-translated copy actively damages brands; long consideration cycles.
- South Korea: Naver + Kakao are core (search + messaging); Instagram strong for
  fashion/beauty; premium CPMs.
- **Mainland China: not addressable with this stack** (Meta/Google/YouTube/TikTok-intl all
  unavailable) — requires a separate WeChat/Douyin/RED/Tmall program.

## Oceania (Australia, New Zealand)
- Premium CPM tier; mechanics similar to UK/US.
- Seasons are inverted (summer = Dec-Feb) — recompute seasonal-sale logic.
- Privacy Act reforms tightening; standard consent + first-party data practice applies.

# Part 3 — Worked examples (full-depth playbooks)

Six markets built to full depth. Use directly if you operate here; otherwise treat as
templates for what the framework should produce.

## Worked example: India — with South India specialization

**Market shape:** ~900M internet users; digital ad spend heading to ~$20B by 2029. 73% of
internet users consume regional-language content (~540M regional-language users). Google +
Meta hold most budget share; ShareChat/Moj, Dailyhunt/Josh, InMobi scale regional inventory.

**Language strategy (the core edge):**
- Regional creative consistently outperforms English outside metro white-collar segments —
  especially tier-2/3 cities.
- South India language map:
  - **Tamil (TN):** Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai + strong diaspora (SG, MY, SL, UK, US).
  - **Telugu (AP + Telangana):** Hyderabad, Vizag, Vijayawada. Big-spending film market.
  - **Kannada (Karnataka):** Bengaluru (mixed English/Kannada); go Kannada beyond the metro.
  - **Malayalam (Kerala):** Kochi, Trivandrum + massive Gulf NRI audience (geo-target GCC
    with Malayalam creative for Kerala brands).
- English VO + regional subtitles is a cheap first step; native regional VO + native creator
  is the real unlock.

**Commerce mechanics:** UPI dominates payments; COD heavy in tier-2/3 (WhatsApp order
confirmation cuts COD returns); click-to-WhatsApp ads convert strongly for considered
purchases; Flipkart Big Billion Days + Amazon Great Indian Festival (Sep-Oct) pull ecom
attention.

**Festival/CPM calendar (South-India-weighted):**
| Window | Event | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Jan (mid) | Pongal / Sankranti | TN/AP/TS/KA retail moment; Tamil film releases cluster |
| Mar-Apr | Ugadi | AP/TS/KA new year |
| Apr (mid) | Tamil New Year / Vishu | TN + Kerala |
| Jul-Aug | Aadi month | TN's traditional discount season |
| Aug-Sep | Onam | Kerala's biggest shopping festival — book 4-6 weeks early |
| Sep-Oct | Navaratri / Dussehra + marketplace sales | CPM climb starts |
| Oct-Nov | **Diwali** | Peak CPMs (2-3x); creative locked 6 weeks ahead |
| Nov-Feb + Apr-Jun | Wedding seasons | Jewelry, clothing, beauty surge |
| Varies | Ramzan/Eid | Kerala, Hyderabad, TN pockets |

**Platform notes:** NO TikTok (banned since June 2020, still banned 2026) — short-video =
Reels + Shorts (+ Moj/Josh managed buys for deep regional reach). YouTube ≈ TV replacement
in every language market. Compliance: DPDP Act (consent), ASCI (influencer disclosure
mandatory), GST on ad spend.

## Worked example: Sri Lanka
~13.9M internet users (~60% penetration), mobile-first. **Facebook IS the internet for the
mass market** (~9M users); YouTube ~8.8M replaced TV for under-35s. **Social commerce >25%
of online sales** via WhatsApp Business + Instagram — build CTWA + IG DM flows, not just
site funnels. Tri-language: Sinhala/Tamil/English (Jaffna Tamil ≠ Chennai Tamil in tone).
Cards under-penetrated; COD + bank transfer common; currency volatility — price in ranges.
Low CPMs — good creative-testing market before scaling to India/SG.

## Worked example: Malaysia
Multilingual: Bahasa Malaysia + English + Chinese + Tamil (Indian-Malaysian diaspora).
TikTok Shop is a major commerce channel — GMV Max + affiliates + Live Shopping. Shopee/
Lazada dominate marketplace mindshare; 11.11/12.12 peaks. **Ramadan → Hari Raya is THE mega
season** — plan Raya creative (family, homecoming) 6-8 weeks out. Payments: Touch 'n Go,
GrabPay, FPX online banking. SEA CPM band = efficient testing + scale market.

## Worked example: Singapore
98%+ penetration, 5.8M users, English-first (+ Chinese, Malay, Tamil official). Premium
market: high CPMs, high AOV, high creative-quality bar (polished UGC). Meta reaches ~75% of
adults; TikTok + IG + YouTube mainstream. PDPA for lead capture; DNC registry for SMS/calls.
Small population → cap frequency, focus LTV/retention; pair acquisition with email/WhatsApp
CRM from day one.

## Worked example: United States
Highest CPMs (Meta often $10-40+; Q4 spikes hard — avoid launch-learning in Nov). UGC/
creator-led table stakes; partnership ads scale. CTV/YouTube big-screen = major surface.
Privacy: ATT, state laws — CAPI + enhanced conversions + first-party data mandatory. TikTok
operates under the post-2025 US joint-venture arrangement — verify status at planning time.
Diaspora lanes: culture-forward creative for immigrant communities works across verticals.

## Worked example: United Kingdom
Mature, expensive, high-standard. **ASA/CAP Code strict** — beauty: no misleading
before/afters, influencer filters can't exaggerate product effect, substantiation required.
GDPR + PECR: Consent Mode v2, CAPI essential. HFSS food ad restrictions. Diaspora
communities significant (Diwali in London is mainstream) — festival-timed campaigns work.

# Cross-geo rules (universal)

1. One language = one ad set/ad group. Always.
2. Test cheap, scale expensive — validate concepts in low-CPM markets when audiences
   overlap (diaspora chains, shared languages).
3. Currency + billing model per account (prepaid vs postpaid, tax registration) checked
   before launch week.
4. Maintain a rolling 90-day festival/peak lookahead for every active client geo.
5. Platform availability re-verified at every planning cycle — bans and restrictions move.
