# Geo Playbooks

## India — with South India specialization

**Market shape:** ~900M internet users; digital ad spend heading to ~$20B by 2029. 73% of
internet users consume regional-language content (~540M regional-language users). Google +
Meta hold most budget share; ShareChat/Moj, Dailyhunt/Josh, InMobi scale regional inventory.

**Language strategy (the core edge):**
- Regional creative consistently outperforms English outside metro white-collar segments —
  especially for tier-2/3, homeowners, non-tech professionals.
- Separate ad sets/ad groups PER LANGUAGE. Never mix languages in one ad set — it muddies
  the creative signal and reporting.
- South India language map:
  - **Tamil (TN):** Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy + strong diaspora (SG, MY, SL, UK, US).
    Tamil creative also works for Tamil diaspora campaigns in Singapore/Malaysia/Sri Lanka.
  - **Telugu (AP + Telangana):** Hyderabad, Vizag, Vijayawada. Big-spending film market.
  - **Kannada (Karnataka):** Bengaluru (mixed English/Kannada), Mysuru. Bengaluru metro
    audiences accept English; go Kannada beyond the metro.
  - **Malayalam (Kerala):** Kochi, Trivandrum, Kozhikode + massive Gulf NRI audience
    (geo-target GCC countries with Malayalam creative for Kerala brands).
- English VO + regional subtitles is a cheap first step; native regional VO + native creator
  is the real unlock.

**Commerce mechanics:**
- UPI dominates payment; COD still heavy in tier-2/3 ecom — plan COD-confirmation flows
  (WhatsApp order confirmation cuts COD returns).
- WhatsApp is a channel, not an afterthought: click-to-WhatsApp ads (CTWA) convert strongly
  for considered purchases and lead gen; pair with WhatsApp Business catalogs.
- Flipkart Big Billion Days + Amazon Great Indian Festival (Sep-Oct) pull ecom attention —
  D2C either rides the wave (own-site sale events) or avoids the CPM peak.

**Festival/CPM calendar (South-India-weighted):**
| Window | Event | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Jan (mid) | Pongal / Sankranti | TN/AP/TS/KA retail moment; Tamil film releases cluster here |
| Mar-Apr | Ugadi / Gudi Padwa | AP/TS/KA new year |
| Apr (mid) | Tamil New Year / Vishu | TN + Kerala |
| Jul-Aug | Aadi month | TN's traditional discount season — retail/fashion sale creative expected |
| Aug-Sep | Onam | Kerala's biggest shopping festival — book 4-6 weeks early |
| Sep-Oct | Navaratri / Dussehra + BBD/GIF sales | CPM climb starts |
| Oct-Nov | **Diwali** | Peak CPMs (2-3x normal); creative locked 6 weeks ahead; highest-intent shopping window |
| Nov-Feb + Apr-Jun | Wedding seasons | Jewelry, clothing, beauty surge |
| Dec | Christmas/New Year | Kerala, Goa, metros |
| Varies | Ramzan/Eid | Significant in Kerala, Hyderabad, TN pockets |

**Platform notes:**
- **NO TIKTOK — banned since June 2020, still banned 2026.** Short-video = Instagram Reels +
  YouTube Shorts (+ Moj/Josh for deep regional reach via managed buys).
- YouTube ≈ TV replacement across language markets; Shorts + regional VO is the reach engine.
- Compliance: DPDP Act (consent for personal data), ASCI code (influencer disclosure
  mandatory), GST on ad spend (GSTIN needed for input credit).

## Sri Lanka

- ~13.9M internet users (~60% penetration), mobile-first.
- **Facebook IS the internet for the mass market** (~9M users) — most cost-effective channel
  for lead gen + community; boosted posts + lead forms perform.
- YouTube ~8.8M users; replaced TV for under-35s.
- **Social commerce >25% of online sales** happens via WhatsApp Business + Instagram — many
  purchases never touch a website. Build CTWA + IG DM flows, not just site funnels.
- Tri-language: Sinhala / Tamil / English. Tamil creative from South India work can be
  adapted (watch dialect + cultural references — Jaffna Tamil ≠ Chennai Tamil in tone).
- Payments: cards under-penetrated; COD + bank transfer common; LKR volatility — price in
  ranges, refresh offers.
- Low CPMs — good market for creative testing before scaling concepts to India/SG.

## Malaysia

- Multilingual: Bahasa Malaysia + English + Chinese + **Tamil (Indian-Malaysian diaspora —
  direct reuse lane for Tamil creative)**.
- TikTok Shop is a major commerce channel — GMV Max + affiliate creators + Live Shopping.
- Marketplaces (Shopee, Lazada) dominate ecom mindshare; 11.11 / 12.12 are peak events.
- **Ramadan → Hari Raya (Aidilfitri) is THE mega season** — fashion/beauty/food budgets
  concentrate here; plan Raya creative (family, homecoming themes) 6-8 weeks out.
- Payments: Touch 'n Go eWallet, GrabPay, online banking (FPX).
- SEA CPM band ($1-5 on TikTok) = efficient testing + scale market.

## Singapore

- 98%+ internet penetration, 5.8M users, English-first (+ Chinese, Malay, Tamil official).
- Premium market: high CPMs, high AOV, high creative-quality bar — polished UGC, not rough UGC.
- Meta reach ~75% of adults; TikTok + IG + YouTube all mainstream.
- Strong card/wallet payments; ecom UX expectations high (fast delivery, easy returns).
- PDPA compliance for lead capture; DNC registry for SMS/phone follow-up.
- Small population → cap frequency, focus LTV and retention; pair acquisition with
  email/WhatsApp CRM from day one.
- Tamil-speaking segment reachable with adapted South-India creative (premium finish).

## United States

- Highest CPMs of our markets (Meta often $10-40+ depending on vertical/season); Q4 CPMs
  spike hard (BFCM) — budget accordingly or avoid launch-learning in Nov.
- UGC/creator-led creative is table stakes; whitelisted creator ads (partnership ads) scale.
- CTV/YouTube on TV screens = major surface; 30-60s cinematic assets warranted.
- Privacy: ATT signal loss on iOS, state privacy laws (CA/CO/VA etc.) — CAPI + enhanced
  conversions + first-party data are mandatory infrastructure, not options.
- TikTok operates under the post-2025 US joint-venture arrangement — active ad platform;
  verify current status at campaign planning time.
- Diaspora angle: Indian-American audiences addressable with culture-forward creative
  (festivals, food, film) — strong lane for skincare/fashion/film clients.

## United Kingdom

- Mature, expensive, high-standard market. Meta + Google + TikTok all mainstream.
- **ASA/CAP Code is strict** — especially beauty/cosmetics: no misleading before/afters,
  filters cannot exaggerate product effect (influencer filter rules), substantiation required
  for efficacy claims. Pre-clear claims before creative production.
- GDPR + PECR: consent-mode v2 on Google stack, cookie consent affects signal — CAPI +
  enhanced conversions essential.
- HFSS product ad restrictions exist (food) — relevant only if food clients onboard.
- South Asian diaspora (significant Tamil + broader Indian communities in London, Midlands)
  — festival-timed campaigns (Diwali in London is mainstream) work for our verticals.

## Cross-geo rules

1. One language = one ad set/ad group. Always.
2. Test cheap (SL/MY), scale expensive (SG/US/UK) — validate concepts in low-CPM markets first
   when the audience overlaps (esp. Tamil diaspora chain: TN → SL/MY/SG → UK/US).
3. Currency + billing model per account (INR prepaid UPI vs USD/GBP postpaid) checked before
   launch week.
4. Festival calendars per geo drive both CPM cost and creative theme — maintain a rolling
   90-day festival lookahead for every active client.
