# Vertical Playbooks

## Skincare / Beauty

**Compliance first — this vertical gets accounts banned.**

Claims language (Meta + Google + ASCI aligned):
- BANNED framing: "treats/cures acne/eczema/psoriasis/rosacea", disease/medical claims,
  guaranteed transformation, "clinically proven" without substantiation on file.
- Meta hard rules: no before/after implying unlikely results; before/after outright
  prohibited for weight-loss and anti-aging/wrinkle products; no negative self-perception
  angles ("your skin is ugly").
- SAFE framing: "helps soothe", "supports", "visibly reduces the appearance of",
  "dermatologically tested" (with test on file), sensory language.
- India ASCI: 500+ beauty brands flagged 2025-26 for exaggerated claims, misleading
  "natural"/"Ayurvedic" use, and missing influencer disclosure. Influencer statements =
  brand claims — the brand answers for them. #ad / #collab disclosure mandatory.
- UK ASA: filters must not exaggerate product effect; efficacy claims need substantiation.
- India regulatory line: cosmetic vs drug (Drugs & Cosmetics Act) — the moment a claim is
  therapeutic, it is a drug claim. Stay cosmetic.

**Strategy:**
- UGC-led: creator testimonials, texture/application ASMR, GRWM formats, derm-adjacent
  authority content. UGC ≈ 24% higher ROAS, ~47-48% lower CPM/higher CTR vs studio.
- A+SC primary + catalog for range sellers; hero-SKU campaigns for the flagship product.
- Ingredient education works in India (turmeric, saffron, coconut, ayurvedic actives) —
  pair heritage ingredient + modern proof point.
- Routine-based bundling (AM/PM routine) lifts AOV; subscription push in US/UK/SG.
- Regional: Malayalam + Tamil beauty creator ecosystems are strong and cheaper than Hindi
  metro creators; Gulf NRI Malayalam audience for Kerala brands.

## Clothing / Fashion

**2026 Meta benchmarks (fashion/apparel):** median ROAS 2.18x; good = 2.2-3.0x; top quartile
4.4x; top decile 6.0x. Judge client performance against these, not vanity targets.

**Structure:**
- 70% budget: catalog-based A+SC on the full catalog (A+SC carries ~62% of Meta ecom spend;
  ~4.52x vs 3.70x manual).
- 30% budget: hero-product / drop campaigns (new collection launches, seasonal stories).
- Catalog retargeting delivers ~4.7x vs ~3.1x prospecting — feed health (images, titles,
  availability, price sync) is a weekly check, not a setup task.

**Creative engine:**
- 21+ new creatives/month minimum; at $5k+/day, 50-100 active creatives and 25-30% refresh
  every 2 weeks.
- Mix: on-model UGC try-ons, flat-lay + motion, street-style, size-inclusive representation,
  fit/fabric close-ups, styling reels ("3 ways to wear").
- India-specific: festival capsule collections (Diwali, Onam, Pongal, Raya for MY) are
  creative moments AND margin moments; wedding-season lookbooks Nov-Feb.
- Sizing + returns clarity in ad/LP reduces CPA and COD refusals (India tier-2/3).

**Seasonality:** BFCM (US/UK), 11.11/12.12 (MY/SG), BBD/GIF + Diwali (India), Raya (MY).
Plan drops against the calendar; never start learning phase inside a peak.

## Film / Entertainment (esp. South Indian cinema)

**Phase-gated campaign arc:**
1. **Announcement/title reveal** — poster + title logo day; fan-army amplification; Google
   SEO/knowledge panel hygiene.
2. **Music-first** (the South Indian edge) — first single launch is its own campaign event;
   audio jukebox + lyric videos on YouTube; Reels/Shorts audio-page seeding so the sound
   trends organically.
3. **Teaser → Trailer** — YouTube masthead-style moments (in-stream + Shorts cutdowns);
   trailer launch as live event; meme-page seeding within hours (memes travel faster than
   promos — supply meme-able frames/dialogues deliberately).
4. **Release week** — FDFS (first day first show) energy content, booking-intent Search
   campaigns ("<title> tickets", BookMyShow/ticketing deep links), celebrity/creator review
   seeding, city-level geo pushes for TN/AP/TS/KA/Kerala markets.
5. **Sustain/OTT window** — dialogue clips, BTS, deleted scenes; OTT-release re-launch
   campaign with platform co-marketing.

**Platform mix:** YouTube 40-50% (trailer views + Shorts), Meta 35-45% (Reels, meme formats,
creator whitelisting), Google Search/Display 10-15% (title + booking intent). India only —
no TikTok; for diaspora (US/UK/SG/MY), TikTok IS in the mix and Tamil/Telugu diaspora
creators drive it.

**Craft notes:**
- Every asset vertical-first (9:16), hook = star/dialogue/music in <2s.
- Language-split everything: Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Hindi dubs each get native
  campaigns — dub trailers are separate assets with separate audiences.
- Influencer tiers: film-review YouTubers + meme pages + micro fan accounts; disclosure still
  applies (ASCI).
- Measure: trailer view velocity (24h/72h), Shorts shares, search volume for title,
  ticketing click-outs. Box-office attribution is directional, not deterministic — set that
  expectation with producers upfront.

## Tech (SaaS / D2C tech / apps)

**B2B SaaS:**
- Budget: Google Search + AI Max 40-50% (high-intent capture), Demand Gen + YouTube 20-30%,
  Meta 20-30% (retargeting, founder-led content, case-study creative).
- Offline conversion import (CRM stage changes) back to Google/Meta is the single biggest
  lever — optimize toward SQL/opportunity, not raw leads.
- Lead forms: DPDP (India) / GDPR (UK) / PDPA (SG) consent language required.
- Longer windows: 30-90 day cycles; judge on pipeline cohorts, not 7-day ROAS.

**Consumer tech / apps:**
- App campaigns (Google UAC, Meta AAA) + event optimization on activation, not install.
- Feature-demo UGC ("watch me do X in 10 seconds") outperforms spec-sheet creative.
- India: price-sensitivity framing (EMI, UPI offers, exchange offers) materially lifts CVR.
